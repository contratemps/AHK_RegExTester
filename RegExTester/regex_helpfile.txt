  Globals
  -----------------------------
  g Greedy
  m Multi-Line
  i Case-Insensitive
  s Dot ALL 
  x ignore whitespace
  A forcse pattern to be anchored
  D force $ to match end of haystack
  U makes it ungreedy
  
  Character classes
  ------------------------------
  .  any character except newline
  \w \d\s  word, digit, whitespace
  \W \D \S  not word, digit, whitespace
  [abc]  any of a, b, or c
  [^abc]  not a, b, or c
  [a-g]  character between a & g
  {min,max} Match a{1,2} matches ab but only the first two a's in aaab {3} matches exactly 3 occurances
  [...]
  [abc] means "any single character that is either a, b or c". 
  
  
  Anchors
  --------------------------------
  ^abc$  start / end of the string
  \b  word boundary
  
  Escaped characters
  ---------------------------------
  \. \* \\  escaped special characters
  \t \n \r  tab, linefeed, carriage return
  \u00A9  unicode escaped Â©
  
  Groups & Lookaround
  -----------------------------------
  (abc)  capture group
  \1  backreference to group #1
  (?:abc)  non-capturing group
  (?=abc)  positive lookahead
  (?!abc)  negative lookahead
  
  Quantifiers & Alternation
  -----------------------------------
  a* a+ a?  0 or more, 1 or more, 0 or 1
  a{5} a{2,}  exactly five, two or more
  a{1,3}  between one & three
  a+? a{2,}?  match as few as possible
  ab|cd  match ab or cd
